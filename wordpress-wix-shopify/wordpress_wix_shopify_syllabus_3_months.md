# WordPress, Wix & Shopify — 3-Month Syllabus

This track assumes **8–10 hours per week**. Each month is roughly **four weeks**. The goal is **job-ready site building** across three common stacks: **WordPress (content/blogs/brochure + WooCommerce intro)**, **Wix (hosted builder)**, and **Shopify (e-commerce)**.

---

## Month 1 — Web Publishing Basics & WordPress Core

- Domains, DNS, hosting types (shared, managed WP), SSL, and backups (conceptual)
- Installing WordPress; admin dashboard tour (posts vs pages, media library)
- Themes: selection criteria, child themes (concept), customization boundaries
- Gutenberg blocks: layouts, reusable blocks, patterns
- Essential plugins mindset: security, caching (intro), forms, SEO plugin (intro)
- **WooCommerce intro (light):** products, categories, payments at a high level
- **Project:** Brochure-style WordPress site (5–7 pages) with blog, contact form, and basic SEO titles/meta

---

## Month 2 — Wix: Builder-First Sites & Client Workflows

- Wix Editor vs Studio (awareness); templates and brand kit basics
- Sections, grids, responsive editing, and mobile preview discipline
- Content management: menus, dynamic pages (intro), SEO panel
- Forms, automations (intro), and integrations (mailing list hookups)
- Client handover: editor permissions, training checklist, and support boundaries
- Compare/contrast: when Wix wins vs WordPress for a given brief
- **Project:** One multi-page Wix site (services business or portfolio) with booking or lead form and analytics connection (conceptual)

---

## Month 3 — Shopify: Store Setup, Merchandising & Launch

- Shopify admin: products, variants, collections, inventory basics
- Themes: customization limits, sections, and performance/common pitfalls
- Checkout, taxes, and shipping concepts (high level; region-dependent)
- Apps: evaluation criteria (cost, support, performance risk)
- Analytics: conversion funnel basics, campaigns parameters (UTM awareness)
- Migration and operations: redirects, 404 handling, launch checklist
- **Capstone:** Functional Shopify demo store (10+ products, collections, policies pages, payment test mode) plus a **comparison doc** (1–2 pages) recommending WP vs Wix vs Shopify for three fictional clients

---

## Outcomes

Graduates should be able to **publish and maintain** a typical **WordPress** site, **ship a polished Wix build** with sensible responsive behavior, and **stand up a credible Shopify store** with products, collections, and a launch-ready checklist.
