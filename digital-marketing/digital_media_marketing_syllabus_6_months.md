# Digital Media Marketing — 6-Month Syllabus

This track assumes **8–10 hours per week** of instruction plus practice. Adjust pacing per cohort; each month is roughly **four weeks**.

---

## Month 1 — Foundations & the Digital Landscape

- Marketing vs digital media: channels, roles, and career paths
- The funnel: awareness → consideration → conversion → retention
- Customer personas, value proposition, and positioning
- Brand voice, messaging hierarchy, and ethical marketing basics
- Introduction to analytics vocabulary (sessions, users, conversions, attribution basics)
- **Project:** Audit one real brand’s digital presence (website + one social channel) and write a one-page brief

---

## Month 2 — Content Strategy & Social Media

- Content pillars, editorial calendars, and repurposing workflows
- Platform overview: short-form video, feeds, stories, professional networks (high level)
- Community management: tone, moderation, crisis awareness
- Basics of visual storytelling (working with designers / stock / simple tools)
- UGC, influencers, and disclosure/compliance awareness
- **Project:** Build a 4-week content calendar with 12 posts and measurable goals

---

## Month 3 — Search & Discovery (SEO + YouTube fundamentals)

- How search engines work; intent types (informational, navigational, transactional)
- On-page SEO: titles, headings, internal links, E-E-A-T concepts (intro)
- Technical SEO intro: sitemaps, crawlability, Core Web Vitals (conceptual)
- Keyword research workflow and content briefs
- YouTube as a search engine: titles, thumbnails, hooks, retention basics
- **Project:** SEO content brief + optimized article or video outline for one keyword cluster

---

## Month 4 — Paid Media & Performance Basics

- Paid vs organic; campaign objectives aligned to business goals
- Meta Ads overview: structure (campaign/ad set/ad), audiences, creatives, pixels (conceptual)
- Google Ads overview: Search vs Performance Max at a high level
- Budgeting, bidding concepts, and frequency/creative fatigue
- Landing page alignment and basic conversion tracking literacy
- **Project:** Mock paid campaign plan with objective, audience, 3 creatives, KPIs, and test matrix

---

## Month 5 — Email, CRM & Marketing Automation (intro)

- Email list hygiene, permissions, and compliance mindset (consent, unsubscribe)
- Lifecycle email: welcome, nurture, promotional, transactional
- Segmentation and personalization patterns (intro)
- CRM concepts: lead status, pipelines, handoff to sales/support
- Simple automation journeys (e.g., abandoned cart concept for e-commerce)
- **Project:** 3-email nurture sequence with subject lines, CTAs, and success metrics

---

## Month 6 — Analytics, Reporting & Capstone

- Google Analytics 4 concepts: events, conversions, audiences (intro)
- Building a monthly performance report: channels, KPIs, insights, next experiments
- A/B testing mindset: hypotheses, sample size awareness, avoiding peeking bias (intro)
- Attribution limitations and incrementality awareness (conceptual)
- Career artifacts: portfolio case study structure (problem → actions → results)
- **Capstone:** End-to-end campaign simulation — strategy deck (8–12 slides), measurement plan, and retrospective

---

## Outcomes

Graduates should be able to **plan integrated digital campaigns**, **produce structured content**, **run introductory paid and organic workflows**, and **report results** with clear next steps.
